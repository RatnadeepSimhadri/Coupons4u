About Coupon4U

Looking for great and exclusive discount coupons of your favorite clothing outlet, restaurant or electronic vendor? Do not worry! Coupons4u gets the latest deals across multiple industries ranging from retail to restaurants. Our dwarf Happy create these specials coupons using his magical powers and delivers these coupons right at your door step (now days on your email too ). Have a happy time shopping! Coupons4U is an ecommerce platform, started in year 2014, by Texas A&M University alumni. Our business spreads across US and Europe, with headquarters being in San Jose, California. We have recently entered the China market, with an aim to capture the Asian market. Our mission is to provide a B2C platform helps connect the enterprises to the customers, with the aims to providing a curated selection of fashion, electronics, restaurant, home furnishing, and many more. Our powerful predictive and prescriptive analytics tool (assisted by our Happy dwarf) provides personalized discount coupons to shoppers by studying their buying history and online behavior.
